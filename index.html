Fonts used in the branding

Momentum Mod uses two different fonts for its branding.

Bebas Neue

This font in it’s Regular weight (the free version of the font) is used in titles, buttons in the game’s UI and on the titlecard itself. Its free version can be downloaded on Google Fonts. It is also available in multiple different versions on GitHub.

How to space the logo

To ensure the best readability of logo, please allow at least 50% of the logo’s width and height around either the logo or the titlecard.

logo use guidelines

Our logo is a very easily recognized aspect of the Momentum Mod brand, and as such should be presented in an easily recognizable form to users around the world.

How to use the logo properly:

It’s important that the appearance of the logo remains consistent. The logo should not be misinterpreted, modified, or added to. Its orientation, color, and composition should remain as indicated in this document — there are no exceptions.

  • Only use the titlecard/logo on one of the backgrounds shown on the right.
  • Do not rotate, deform or modify the logo in any aspect.
  • Keep the Momentum Blue, Grey and White while using the logo.
  • Do not change the logo color or tone outside of the brand colors.
  • Do not apply a gradient to the logo or wordmark.
  • Do not remove the outline stroke.
